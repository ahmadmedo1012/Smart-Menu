# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Restaurant owners** — create and manage digital menus, track orders, set pricing, manage QR codes, view analytics. Their primary workflow is the owner dashboard and menu management.

**Customers** — browse menus, place orders, pay, earn loyalty points. Their primary flow is scan → browse → order → pay.

**Admin team** (platform operator) — manage users, restaurants, subscriptions, audit logs, system settings via admin panel.

## Product Purpose

Smart Menu replaces printed restaurant menus with a complete digital ordering platform. Restaurant owners create QR-linked digital menus; customers scan, browse, and order from their phones — no app install needed. The platform handles the full order lifecycle: menu display, cart management, order submission, payment processing, and loyalty/referral system.

Success: every restaurant that signs up keeps using it daily. Customers open menus without friction.

## Brand Personality

**Warm · Premium · Trusted**

Hospitality-driven design with the warmth of a great restaurant welcome. Gold/amber tones signal quality and taste. Clean glass surfaces and refined typography convey trust and professionalism. Arabic-first with global-ready bilingual support.

Voice: confident but never loud, premium but not pretentious, warm without being casual.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS templates (identical card grids, numbered section markers, tiny uppercase eyebrows)
- Cold corporate blue/gray dashboards that feel clinical
- Over-animated food apps that distract from the menu
- Dark patterns or urgency tricks in ordering flow
- "AI-generated" design tells: gradient text, glassmorphism as default, side-stripe borders

## Design Principles

1. **The menu is sacred** — nothing distracts from the menu itself. Typography, spacing, and imagery serve readability and choice.
2. **Trust through polish** — every pixel communicates reliability. Restaurants trust us with their revenue; the interface must feel bank-grade.
3. **Hospitality translates** — warmth is not color alone. It's rhythm, space, timing, and micro-copy that says "we've got you."
4. **Two registers, one voice** — the marketing surface (landing/pricing) is warmer and more aspirational; the product surface (menu/dashboard) is quieter and more functional. Both speak the same brand language.
5. **Speed is a feature** — load fast, render instantly, pay in seconds. Motion enhances understanding; it never delays it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.2 AA minimum
- Full RTL support for Arabic (primary language for owners)
- Reduced motion respected at system level
- High-contrast mode compatible
- Touch-optimized targets for restaurant floor use
- Legible at arm's length on a phone in a dim restaurant
