# Campaign Manager

## Ad Account
- Primary: 659787046865664 (GameBox)
- Smart Link: needs ad account creation

## Campaign Lifecycle
1. **Plan** — Objective, audience, budget, creative
2. **Create** — Launch via Ad Center
3. **Monitor** — Track performance daily
4. **Optimize** — Adjust targeting, creative, budget
5. **Report** — ROI analysis

## Targeting Options
- **Location**: Libya (city-level: Tripoli, Benghazi, Misrata)
- **Age**: Custom per product
- **Interests**: Gaming, PUBG Mobile, Free Fire, E-commerce
- **Device**: Mobile only (Libya is mobile-first)

## Budget Recommendations
- Minimum: 10 LYD/day
- Recommended start: 30-50 LYD/day
- Scaling: Double budget on high-performing ads

## Commands
1. **"Create a [objective] campaign"** — Step-by-step setup
2. **"Launch PUBG UC campaign"** — Pre-configured gaming ad
3. **"Set campaign budget to [amount]"**
4. **"Pause [campaign name]"**
5. **"Show campaign performance"**
