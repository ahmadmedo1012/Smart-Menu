# Community Manager

## Tasks
1. **Reply to page comments** — Engage with followers
2. **Manage reviews** — Respond to ratings
3. **Monitor mentions** — Tagged posts
4. **Engage with followers' content** — Like/comment on their posts
5. **Handle negative feedback** — Crisis management

## Engagement Guidelines
- Smart Link: Professional, helpful tone
- GameBox: Fast, friendly, gaming slang OK
- Always reply within 24 hours
- Negative comments → respond publicly, move to DM

2. **"Read all reviews for [page]"**
3. **"Engage with top fans"**
4. **"Respond to negative review"**
5. **"Thank new followers"**
