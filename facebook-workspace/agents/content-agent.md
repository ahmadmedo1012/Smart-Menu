## Commands
1. **"Create [N] posts for this week"** — Generate N posts with images, schedule them
2. **"Post [message] to [page]"** — Publish immediately
3. **"Create a Story about [topic]"** — Design + publish a story
4. **"Schedule [message] for [date/time]"** — Use content planner
5. **"Post a Reel about [topic]"** — Create short video content
